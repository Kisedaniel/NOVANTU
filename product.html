Novantu is the takeoff, the bid leveling matrix, the outreach, and the report — built to move a set of plans into a sent bid in one sitting.

CORE FEATURES

Six tools. One workflow.

Each piece hands off to the next — plans in, bid out.

AI

Takeoff Assist

Trade calculators turn your measurements into a priced, itemized budget. For countable scope — HVAC equipment, sprinkler heads, electrical fixtures — the AI drafts a first-pass count from the plan set into an editable sheet you review line by line. Area-based measurement (sf/sy) isn't reliable yet, so we don't recommend it for those scopes.

Bid Leveling Matrix

Compare every subcontractor's bid side by side, line by line, so nothing hides inside a lump-sum number.

Sub Directory & Outreach

Keep your trusted subs organized by trade and region, then invite the whole list to bid in a single click — sent from your own email, so it lands in their inbox, not their spam.

Follow-ups & Tracking

Novantu tracks who has viewed, bid or gone quiet, and drafts the follow-up from your own template — you review it and send in one click.

Estimate Builder

Turn the winning bids into a clean estimate with your markup applied, ready to send to the client or owner.

AI

Ask Novantu

Ask a plain-language question about the plan set — room counts, load-bearing walls, revisions — and get an answer with the exact sheet cited.
