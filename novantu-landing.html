Terms & services

What you're paying for — in plain language.

No fine print games. Here's exactly what the subscription includes, how billing works, and who you're contracting with today.

What's included

  • A white-labeled NOVANTU workspace under your brand, on the plan you choose (Standard, Pro or Enterprise).
  • A one-time personalized set-up with your first account: we configure your logo, trade categories and unit prices so you can quote on day one.
  • Software updates and email support for as long as your subscription is active.

Billing & cancellation

  • Plans are billed monthly or annually (annual is paid once for the year). Prices are in USD.
  • Pricing differs by account type: a general contractor's Pro/Enterprise costs more than a subcontractor's because GCs use more storage (more projects & plan files) and more AI. Standard is a flat $200 for both.
  • The one-time set-up fee is charged once, on your first billing only. The checkout asks you to confirm whether it's already been paid.
  • Cancel anytime. Your access continues to the end of the period already paid; we don't pro-rate partial months. No long-term contract.

Your data & privacy

  • Your projects, prices and client data are yours. We don't sell them or share them between workspaces — each shop's data is isolated.
  • You can request an export or deletion of your workspace data at any time.

Who you're contracting with

NOVANTU is currently being formalized as a registered business — that process is underway and not yet complete. Once it's finalized, this agreement and your subscription carry over to the registered entity automatically, with no change to your price. In the meantime, payments are processed securely via Payoneer and your receipts are issued directly to you. This page is a plain-language summary, not legal or tax advice.
