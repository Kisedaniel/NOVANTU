BidFlow — Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how BidFlow ("BidFlow", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the BidFlow web application (the "Service"). BidFlow is a construction estimating and project management tool used by general contractors and their teams.

1. Information we collect

BidFlow stores the information you choose to enter into the Service, including:

  • Account details: email address and display name.
  • Workspace and project data: projects, subcontractors, estimates, bids, plans, schedules, daily logs, change orders, and similar construction records that you create or upload.
  • Files you upload, such as plan PDFs and bid PDFs.
  • Google account information you authorize (see "Google user data" below).

2. How we use the information

We use the information solely to operate the Service for you: storing your records, displaying them in the app, and performing the actions you request (such as sending emails on your behalf, or running AI analyses you initiate).

We do not sell your data, and we do not use your data to train AI models.

3. Google user data

If you connect a Google account to BidFlow, we request the following Google scope:

  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/gmail.send — to send emails on your behalf from your connected Gmail account, only when you trigger a send action in BidFlow (for example, sending an RFQ or a client update).

We store a Google refresh token for your workspace on our server (Supabase) so we can send emails on your behalf. We do not read your inbox, do not access existing messages, and only send messages that you initiate through BidFlow.

You can revoke BidFlow's access at any time from your Google Account: https://myaccount.google.com/permissions. You can also disconnect Gmail from inside BidFlow's Workspace settings.

BidFlow's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

4. How we store and protect data

Application data and uploaded files are stored on Supabase. Sensitive credentials such as Google refresh tokens are kept server-side and are not exposed to your browser. We follow reasonable industry practices to protect against unauthorized access, but no system is perfectly secure.

5. Third-party services

BidFlow uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Supabase — database, authentication, file storage, serverless functions.
  • Google Gmail API — sending emails from your connected Gmail (only when you trigger it).
  • Anthropic (Claude) — AI features such as bid leveling, plan revision diff, and weekly client update drafting. Content is processed by Anthropic only to fulfill the request and is not used to train models.

6. Data retention and deletion

We keep your data as long as your account or workspace is active. You can delete projects, files, and entire workspaces from inside the app, which removes them from our database and storage. To delete your account entirely, contact us at the email below.

7. Children

BidFlow is intended for professional construction use. The Service is not directed to children under 16.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.
